# Outreach templates

Cold-outreach scripts for the targets this pipeline surfaces. Replace every
`{{placeholder}}` with your own details before sending. Keep a few framings ready
and pick one per target — see the cheat sheet at the bottom.

---

## Cold email (~140 words)

**Subject** — pick one:
- `{{your_specialty}} → curious about {{Company}}`
- `{{your_role}} — wanted to reach out`

**Body**

Hi {{Name}},

I'm {{your_name}} — {{your_current_role}} at {{your_company}}. {{one_line_on_what_you_do}}

Recently I {{quantified_win_1}}, and before that {{quantified_win_2}}.

{{Company}}'s work on {{specific_thing}} maps directly to what I do. Could you
point me to the right person on the {{team}} team, or let me know if there's an
open role?

{{your_name}}
{{your_contact_line}}

---

## Short DM (~55 words)

> Hi — I'm {{your_name}}, {{your_role}}. Recently {{quantified_win_1}} and
> {{quantified_win_2}}. Interested in what {{Company}} is building — any
> {{role_family}} roles open? {{cv_or_profile_link}}

---

## Pick-the-frame cheat sheet

| Target type | Lead with |
|---|---|
| Client-facing / FDE roles | your forward-deployed framing + your most relevant client win |
| Infra / platform roles | the systems you run + a scale/throughput number |
| Data / warehouse roles | a pipeline you shipped + a cost/latency number |
| Frontier labs | mix framings; note the seniority you're targeting |
| Regional companies | lead with the win that maps to their domain |

---

## Tips

- Always include concrete numbers — they do the convincing.
- Keep one or two signature technical specifics that prove you actually run the stack.
- Trim words before you trim numbers.
